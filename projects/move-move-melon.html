Move Move Melon!

Role: Designer / Programmer • Unity • 2024

Overview

Move Move Melon! is a fast-paced arcade prototype focused on precision timing, juicy feedback, and clear player communication. The game challenges players to chain moves in rhythm and react to dynamic obstacles in quick bursts of action.

My Contributions

  • Designed and implemented the core gameplay loop in Unity.
  • Created responsive controls with tight input buffering for precision.
  • Polished the player experience with “juicy” audio-visual feedback.
  • Conducted rapid playtesting sessions to balance difficulty and pacing.
